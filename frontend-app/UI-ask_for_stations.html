Welcome to the train arrival predictor!

This tool uses a machine learning model to predict if the train you plan to buy a ticket for will arrive on time at the final station.

Find your departure at sj.se
https://www.sj.se/en/kop-resa/sok/resa/fran-station/till-station/datum
Write for your chosen departure "From", "To", "Date" and "Time" of departure.
On time is by Trafikverket's definition max 5 min 59 sek late.
